Breakthrough Outreach Shelter Network Style Guide

Breakthrough Outreach Shelter Network is a 501(c)(3) whose mission is to end homelessness, restore hope and preserve the environment using technology, innovation, collaboration and compassion.

Brand Targets:

Corporate, small business and religious group donors/volunteers, the clients they serve and individuals who are interested in helping to end homelessness.

Brand Character:

Is caring,hopeful, intelligent, community network minded, barrier breaking, supportive and welcoming to all.

Theme:

Shattering expectations and misconceptions. Breaking through barriers, whether those barriers come in the form of communication, financial, mental or physical impediments. A new day to breakthrough and start anew.
